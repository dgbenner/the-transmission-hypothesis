# Data Files

Research catalogs for historical film footage and broadcast signal intrusions.

## Raw Data

### early_film_footage_1900s-1920s.ods
30 examples of raw film recordings from the 1900s, 1910s, and 1920s featuring:
- Street scenes without narration or focal points
- Public spaces with pedestrians, cars, everyday life
- Silent footage from major cities (NYC, London, Paris, San Francisco, Chicago, Berlin)
- Public domain sources from Internet Archive, Prelinger Archives

### pirate_broadcast_intrusions.ods
30 examples of broadcast signal hijackings and pirate TV/radio intrusions:
- Max Headroom incidents (1987)
- Captain Midnight HBO hijacking (1986)
- Southern Television "Vrillon" alien message (1977)
- EAS zombie apocalypse hacks (2013, 2017)
- Various international signal intrusions

## Format

All files are ODS (OpenDocument Spreadsheet) format compatible with:
- LibreOffice Calc
- Microsoft Excel
- Google Sheets (import)

### Column Schema
- **Title** - Descriptive name of the content
- **Link** - URL to video/article/documentation
- **Description** - What the footage/incident shows
- **Tags** - Comma-separated keywords (era, location, type, etc.)

## Sources

- Internet Archive (archive.org)
- Prelinger Archives
- Wikipedia broadcast intrusion articles
- Public domain film collections
- Historical broadcast documentation

## Usage

These catalogs serve as research material for content aggregation and curation projects. All linked content is either public domain or documented historical incidents.
